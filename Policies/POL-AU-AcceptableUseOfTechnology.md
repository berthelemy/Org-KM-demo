## Purpose

Define the standards for responsible, secure use of all company-provided technology and systems, including remote working tools.

## Scope

Applies to: telephony systems, CRM platforms, desktops/laptops, headsets, chat and messaging tools, email, internet access, and any personal device used to access company systems (BYOD).

## Authorised Use

All company technology must be used **primarily for business purposes**. Incidental personal use is permitted where it does not:
- Affect productivity or availability during shift
- Consume excessive bandwidth or storage
- Access prohibited content categories (see below)
- Compromise the security of company or customer data

## Prohibited Activities

The following are prohibited at all times, including outside work hours on company devices:

- Accessing, downloading, or distributing illegal, offensive, discriminatory, or adult content
- Installing unauthorised software or browser extensions
- Disabling security controls (antivirus, VPN, screen lock)
- Sharing login credentials or using another team member's credentials
- Using company systems to run personal business ventures
- Accessing customer data outside of an active, authorised work interaction
- Recording calls outside of the authorised call recording system

## Remote Working — Additional Controls

- Company VPN must be active whenever accessing internal systems
- A private, password-protected Wi-Fi network must be used (public Wi-Fi is not permitted without VPN)
- Screens must not be visible to unauthorised persons during customer interactions
- Company devices must be locked when unattended

## Social Media (Work Hours)

- Personal social media use during shift is not permitted
- Team members may not post content that references customers, clients, internal processes, or colleagues in a way that could cause harm or embarrassment
- Official company social media accounts may only be managed by authorised team members

## Monitoring

The company reserves the right to monitor use of company systems for security and compliance purposes. Monitoring is proportionate and conducted in accordance with the [POL-DP-DataProtectionAndGDPR](POL-DP-DataProtectionAndGDPR.md) and applicable law. Team members are informed of monitoring at the point of system access.

## Non-Compliance

Breaches of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including dismissal, and in cases involving data protection, may be referred to the ICO or law enforcement.
