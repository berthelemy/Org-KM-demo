## Purpose

Support all team members to meet and sustain the performance standards required to deliver excellent customer service.

## Core Call Centre KPIs

| KPI | Description | Typical Target Range |
| ----- | ------------- | ---------------------- |
| AHT (Average Handle Time) | Mean duration of a call including wrap-up | Varies by contact type |
| CSAT (Customer Satisfaction) | Post-call survey score | ≥ 85% |
| FCR (First Contact Resolution) | Issue resolved without callback | ≥ 80% |
| Quality Score | Call monitoring / evaluation score | ≥ 90% |
| Schedule Adherence | % of shift spent in correct state | ≥ 90% |
| Conversion Rate | Sales / upsell targets where applicable | Per campaign targets |

## Performance Review Cycle

- **Weekly**: Team Leader 1:1 — informal KPI review
- **Monthly**: Formal scorecard review, documented in HR system
- **Quarterly**: Development review — goals, training needs, career progression
- **Annually**: Full appraisal with pay review eligibility

## Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)

Triggered when a team member falls below target on two or more KPIs for **two consecutive months**:

1. Team Leader and HR agree PIP objectives and timescales (typically 4–8 weeks)
2. PIP document signed by team member and Team Leader
3. Weekly check-in meetings held and documented
4. At PIP end: **passed** (return to standard review cycle), **extended** (exceptional circumstances), or **escalated** (disciplinary)

## Manager Responsibilities

- Set SMART targets aligned to team and site goals
- Provide coaching and call listening feedback weekly
- Document all performance conversations in the HR system
- Raise concerns early — do not wait for the annual appraisal

## Employee Rights

- Request a copy of any documented performance record
- Be accompanied by a colleague or trade union rep at formal performance reviews
- Appeal a PIP outcome through the Grievance procedure
