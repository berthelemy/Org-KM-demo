## Purpose

Ensure all team members arrive on time, adhere to their scheduled start times, and maintain reliable attendance to support service level targets.

## Key Standards

- Team members must be **logged in and ready to take calls** by their scheduled start time (not merely on-site)
- Lateness of **more than 5 minutes** must be reported to the Team Leader before the shift starts
- **Three or more late arrivals** within a rolling 12-week period triggers an informal discussion; further instances escalate to formal process
- Unplanned absence must be reported by **phone call** (not text/email) to the direct Team Leader at least **30 minutes before shift start**
- Persistent short-term absence (e.g. Bradford Factor score >200) triggers a return-to-work interview

## Manager Actions

- Log all lateness and absence in the workforce management system
- Conduct return-to-work interviews on the first day back after any absence
- Escalate patterns to HR after trigger thresholds are reached

## Bradford Factor Guidance

$$B = S^2 \times D$$
Where $S$ = number of separate absences; $D$ = total days absent

| Score | Action |
| ------- | -------- |
| < 50 | No action |
| 50–199 | Informal discussion |
| 200–399 | Formal stage 1 |
| 400+ | Formal stage 2 / potential dismissal |
