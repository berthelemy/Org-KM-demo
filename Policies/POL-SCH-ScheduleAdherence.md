## Purpose

Maximise the availability of team members in line with forecast demand, protecting service levels and team fairness.

## Definition

Schedule Adherence measures the percentage of time a team member is in the correct system state (ready, on a call, in wrap, on authorised break) versus their scheduled state.

$$\text{Adherence \%} = \frac{\text{Time in scheduled state}}{\text{Total scheduled time}} \times 100$$

## Target

- Minimum adherence target: **90%**
- Teams below 85% site-wide will trigger a workforce management review

## Scheduled States

| State | Description |
| ------- | ------------- |
| Ready | Logged in and available for inbound contacts |
| On Contact | Actively handling a call, chat, or email |
| Wrap / ACW | Post-contact admin (After Call Work) |
| Authorised Break | Scheduled break, lunch, or coaching session |
| Training | On approved training activity |
| Meeting | Authorised team brief or 1:1 |
| Aux / Unavailable | Unscheduled off-phone time — monitored closely |

## Breaks and Scheduling

- Break times are allocated by the Workforce Management team based on call volume forecasts
- Team members must not change break times unilaterally
- Requests to swap breaks must be submitted to the Team Leader **before** the shift begins
- Comfort breaks are permitted outside scheduled breaks and are not penalised in adherence scoring

## Non-Adherence

- Adherence below 85% in a week triggers an informal conversation with the Team Leader
- Persistent non-adherence (below target for 3+ consecutive weeks) is managed under the [POL-PERF-PerformanceManagement](POL-PERF-PerformanceManagement.md)
- Deliberate avoidance of calls (e.g. extended aux, repeated early log-off) may be treated as misconduct

## Shift Pattern Changes

- Requests for permanent shift changes must be made in writing to HR (minimum 4 weeks notice)
- Temporary shift swaps between team members must be approved by the Team Leader in advance
- Rotational shift patterns are published a minimum of 2 weeks in advance

## Manager Responsibilities

- Monitor real-time adherence dashboards and intervene promptly when agents are out of adherence
- Record discussions about repeated non-adherence in the HR system
- Provide feedback on adherence as part of weekly 1:1s
